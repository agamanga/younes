Story of Manga

In 1991, the tourist town of Morocco ”Agadir” recovered slowly from the devastating earthquake 1960. The built of airport that thousands of travelers passing through the customs clearance gate every day until appear a passenger has a passport. The strange thing is no one knows that country who issued that passport, the passenger claims to came from a country called “Tibetania”. they think that he is a liar so they detained at the police station to investigate with him, in the prison he meet a boy called ounka ...>
